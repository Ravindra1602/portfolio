Professional Profile

I am Ravindra Andukuri, a Mechanical Engineering professional with extensive experience in CAD/CAM, laboratory development, intelligent manufacturing, condition monitoring, predictive maintenance, and AI-driven engineering applications. My research integrates artificial intelligence, fuzzy multi-criteria decision-making, reliability engineering, and smart manufacturing to develop practical solutions for industrial challenges.

Over the years, I have contributed to research publications, patent development, consultancy projects, laboratory modernization, and technical mentoring. My academic interests focus on translating engineering research into reliable industrial technologies that improve productivity, sustainability, and maintenance decision-making.

This website presents my research activities, publications, patents, books, consultancy projects, laboratory initiatives, and other professional contributions.

"Engineering research creates the greatest impact when scientific knowledge is transformed into practical solutions that improve industrial reliability, sustainability, and intelligent decision-making."
